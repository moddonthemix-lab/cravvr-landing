# Month 1 Ad Copy — Truck Operator Acquisition

3 variants per city across Meta Lead Ads + Google Search. Pair with the
landing URLs in §4. UTMs are baked in — copy directly into Ads Manager.

Naming convention for `utm_content` (creative_id):
- `m1` = month 1 campaign
- `prt|stp|tpa` = city
- `a|b|c` = creative variant letter
- Example: `m1-prt-a` = Month 1, Portland, variant A

## Google Search — exact match (one ad group per city)

**Keywords (all three cities):**
- `food truck pos system [city]`
- `how to get more food truck customers [city]`
- `food truck app for owners`
- `mobile ordering for food trucks`

**Negative keywords:** jobs, hiring, for sale, rental, wedding, party, catering

### Ad copy (3 headlines × 2 descriptions, RSA format)
**Headlines (15-char max each):**
1. More Truck Orders
2. Free Food Truck App
3. Cravvr for Trucks
4. 0% Commission Pickup
5. Get Listed Today

**Descriptions (90-char max):**
1. Put your truck on Cravvr — hungry locals nearby see you in real time. Free to join.
2. Mobile ordering, no commission on pickup, zero setup cost. Apply in 60 seconds.

**Final URL:**
`https://cravvr.com/for-trucks/{city}?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=m1-truck-onboard&utm_content={creative}&utm_term={keyword}`
